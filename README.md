## SYNX TECH – Quarterly Team Performance Dashboard
An interactive 3-page Power BI dashboard that helps sales managers track team performance, monitor agent productivity, and analyze product-wise sales trends across quarters — enabling faster, data-driven decisions.

# 1.Business Problem
Sales managers at SYNX Tech had no centralized way to monitor their team's quarterly performance, track individual agent productivity, or identify which products were winning or losing in the market — making strategic decisions slow and data-blind.

# 2.Goal of the Dashboard
To build an interactive, drill-down dashboard that helps sales managers instantly answer:
- How is my team performing vs last quarter?
- Which agents are underperforming?
- Which products have the highest conversion rates?
- How are sales trending across all 4 quarters?

# 3.Key Visuals
📌 Page 1 – Home (Quarterly Snapshot)
- KPI cards: Total Sales, Avg. Sale Value, Avg. Weeks to Close,New Opportunities, Open Deals — each compared to last quarterand all-teams average
- Performance by Agent table with Win % per agent
- Sales & Conversion by Product with revenue bars

 📌 Page 2 – Current Quarter Sales Breakdown
- Total Sales by Sector (horizontal bar chart)
- Total Sales by Product (column chart)
- Total Sales by Account (donut chart)
- Sales Detail drill-down table with agent, account, product,close date, and total sales

📌 Page 3 – Performance Overtime
- Total Sales by Quarter trend line (Q1–Q4)
- New Opportunities by Quarter stacked bar (Won/Lost/Engaging)
- Total Sales by Sales Agent ranked bar
- Conversion Rate by Product per Quarter heatmap table

- # 4.Key Insights
- 📉 Total Sales dropped from $768K (Q3) to $671K in Q4,signaling a slowdown that needs attention
- 🏆 Darcel Schlecht is the top agent with $1.15M annual sales —nearly 3x the next agent
- 🎯 Gtx Plus Pro has the highest Win Rate at 67.5%,yet low sales volume — an upselling opportunity
- 📦 Gtxpro drives the most revenue ($3.56K won sales)but conversion dipped to 55.2% in Q4
- 🏭 Technology & Marketing are the top-performing sectors

# 5.Business Impact
This dashboard empowers sales managers to:
- ✅ Spot underperforming agents early and offer coaching
- ✅ Shift focus to high-conversion products like Gtx Plus Pro
- ✅ Prioritize Technology & Marketing sectors for Q1 planning
- ✅ Reduce avg. weeks-to-close (currently 6.25) by identifyingfaster-closing agents and replicating their approach

 # 6.Tools Used
📊 Power BI Desktop – Main data visualization platform used for report creation.
🗂️ Power Query – Data transformation and cleaning layer for reshaping and preparing the sales data.
🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Used for calculated measures, dynamic KPIs, and conditional logic.
🔗 Data Modeling – Relationships established among tables (sales, products, accounts, and agents) to enable cross-filtering and aggregation.
📁 File Format – .pbix for development and .png for dashboard previews.

# 7.Data Source
Source: Maven Analytics
Data on quarterly sales transactions for a fictional tech company (SYNX Tech), including details on sales agents, product categories, customer accounts, sectors, deal close dates, and win/loss outcomes — structured and modeled within Power BI Desktop.
